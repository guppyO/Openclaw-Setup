# Experiment: Repurposing engine for expert-led content portfolios

## Thesis

Target one expert persona, produce a tightly defined monthly repurposing package, and operationalize delivery around deterministic checklists.

## Opportunity

- Opportunity: Repurposing engine for expert-led content portfolios
- Lane family: service-plus-software
- Budget cap: $225
- Review date: 2026-03-17

## Launch checklist

- Define asset package and offer page
- Attach analytics and evidence capture
- Confirm treasury category and spend tags
- Create kill and compound review dates

## Measurement plan

- Capture spend, attributable revenue, and response rate daily
- Review conversion and payback time every 24 hours during launch week
- Update portfolio score after each milestone or anomaly

## Thresholds

- Kill: Kill if no meaningful traction signal appears before 60% of budget cap is consumed.
- Compound: Compound if initial payback is visible and distribution can be expanded without sharply raising CAC.
